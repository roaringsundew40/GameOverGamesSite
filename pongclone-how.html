How to play PongClone

December 27th, 2020

Playing PongClone is very simple thanks to the easy interface of itch.io. Itch.io is a site which offers free game hosting for small game devs (like me). Follow the directions below to download PongClone for your Operating System.

Pong is a table tennis-themed arcade video game, featuring simple two-dimensional graphics, manufactured by Atari and originally released in 1972. It was one of the earliest arcade video games.

Before continuing open a browser of your choice and go to this link

Before Beginning choose if you want an AI Version or a Non-AI version (which is referred to as local on the game). The AI version is basically the version in which Player2 is an AI. The Non-AI Version which is known as Local is where Player1 and Player2 are both on the same computer playing together. Then follow the instructions for your specific Operating System (OS)
Windows (Requires Administrator Approval): Download the .msi based on your choice of AI and Non-AI. When you have downloaded the .msi, run it. Follow the installation steps. After that, search for PongClone in your Search Bar. You should see it. Click on it and run.
Mac: Download the .zip file based on your choice of Ai and Non-AI. Then unzip the file. After unzipping put the .app file into your Applications Directory. Now use the search bar and search for PongClone. Once you find it click on it and run.
You can play the game in the web, too.
